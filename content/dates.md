Please note the following critical milestones and deadlines for IPAC'28. We recommend marking these dates in your calendar.

{{< timeline >}}
    {{< timeline-item date="October 1, 2027" badge="Open" badge_class="badge-success" title="Abstract Submission & Exhibitor Booking Opens" >}}
    Authors can begin submitting abstracts via the JACoW Indico portal. Industry partners can start reserving booths in the exhibition hall.
    {{< /timeline-item >}}
    
    {{< timeline-item date="December 5, 2027" badge="Critical" badge_class="badge-danger" title="Abstract Submission Deadline" >}}
    **Abstract submission closes strictly at 23:59 JST.** No late submissions or modifications will be processed.
    {{< /timeline-item >}}
    
    {{< timeline-item date="January 10, 2028" badge="Info" badge_class="badge-default" title="Conference Registration & Student Grant Applications Open" >}}
    Early bird registration rate becomes active. Eligible students can apply for the IPAC'28 Travel Grant.
    {{< /timeline-item >}}
    
    {{< timeline-item date="February 15, 2028" badge="Milestone" badge_class="badge-default" title="Author Notification & Student Grant Deadline" >}}
    Abstract decisions (Oral, Poster, or Rejection) are communicated to primary authors. Student grant applications close.
    {{< /timeline-item >}}
    
    {{< timeline-item date="April 1, 2028" badge="Action" badge_class="badge-danger" title="Early Registration Rate Closes" >}}
    Last day to register under the discounted early-bird rate. After this date, standard conference registration fees apply.
    {{< /timeline-item >}}
    
    {{< timeline-item date="May 20, 2028" badge="Deadline" badge_class="badge-danger" title="Final Paper Submission Deadline" >}}
    All contributions must upload their final, peer-review-ready PDF and source files (LaTeX/Word) to the JACoW Scientific Repository for editorial checks.
    {{< /timeline-item >}}
    
    {{< timeline-item date="June 4, 2028" badge="Event" badge_class="badge-success" title="Pre-Registration & Welcome Drink" >}}
    Early badge pick-up and registration desk opens at the Tokyo International Forum, followed by the informal Welcome Drink in the evening.
    {{< /timeline-item >}}
    
    {{< timeline-item date="June 5-9, 2028" badge="Conference" badge_class="badge-success" title="IPAC'28 Scientific Program" >}}
    Timetable sessions, plenary keynotes, parallel talks, commercial exhibitions, and the official social banquet.
    {{< /timeline-item >}}
{{< /timeline >}}
