> The student program for IPAC'28 will be announced closer to the conference date. Please check back regularly for updates.

IPAC'28 places a major emphasis on education and professional growth for the next generation of accelerator scientists and engineers. A highly rich, dedicated Student Program has been organized:

### Key Activities

- **Student Seminar (Sunday, June 4)**  
  A series of tutorial-style lectures delivered by senior researchers covering essential accelerator fundamentals and novel technologies. Free to attend for all registered student grant recipients.
  
- **Poster Session & Judging**  
  Students have the opportunity to present their research in a dedicated poster session slot and compete for the prestigious **Outstanding Student Poster Awards**.
  
- **Student Reception**  
  An informal networking dinner hosted in Yurakucho on Monday evening, letting students mingle, discuss career paths with industry experts, and establish global peer connections.
