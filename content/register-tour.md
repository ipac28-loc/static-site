A dedicated post-conference Technical Tour is organized for Friday, June 9 (afternoon) and Saturday, June 10, allowing participants to visit Japan's world-leading particle accelerator facilities and national scientific laboratories.

### Tour Options

- **Option A: KEK - High Energy Accelerator Research Organization (Tsukuba)**
  *Schedule*: Saturday, June 10 (Full Day)
  *Description*: Visit the SuperKEKB collider, the Belle II detector, the Photon Factory synchrotron, and advanced superconducting cavities fabrication facilities.
  *Capacity*: 100 participants (Pre-registration required).
  *Fee*: `To be added` (Includes shuttle bus and lunch box).

- **Option B: J-PARC - Japan Proton Accelerator Research Complex (Tokai)**
  *Schedule*: Saturday, June 10 (Full Day)
  *Description*: Tour the high-intensity proton linac, the 3 GeV Rapid Cycling Synchrotron (RCS), the Main Ring (MR), the Materials and Life Science Experimental Facility (MLF), and the Neutrino Beamline.
  *Capacity*: 80 participants.
  *Fee*: `To be added`.

- **Option C: RIKEN Nishina Center for Accelerator-Based Science (Wako)**
  *Schedule*: Friday, June 9 (Afternoon)
  *Description*: Visit the Radioactive Isotope Beam Factory (RIBF), home of the world's most powerful heavy-ion superconducting ring cyclotron.
  *Capacity*: 60 participants.
  *Fee*: `To be added`.
