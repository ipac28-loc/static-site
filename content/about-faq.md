### Registration & Grants

{{< accordion title="When will registration open and what are the fees?" >}}
Registration is scheduled to open in **January 2028**. Early registration discounts will be available until **April 1, 2028**. Detailed registration tiers (Regular, Student, Exhibitor) will be announced shortly.
{{< /accordion >}}

{{< accordion title="Are there grants available for students?" >}}
Yes, a robust Student Grant Program will be available to help cover registration fees, economy travel, and shared accommodation in Tokyo. Applications will open alongside registration and close in **February 2028**.
{{< /accordion >}}

### Visas & Travel

{{< accordion title="Do I need a visa to enter Japan?" >}}
Many countries have visa-exempt agreements with Japan. However, nationals of certain countries must obtain a visa before entering. Please check the Official Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA) website or refer to our [Visa Information](/visa/) page.
{{< /accordion >}}

{{< accordion title="Will the conference provide visa support letters?" >}}
Yes! The Local Organizing Committee will provide official visa support documentation (invitation letter, guarantee letter, etc.) *only* to registered and fully paid attendees, presenters, and exhibitors.
{{< /accordion >}}
